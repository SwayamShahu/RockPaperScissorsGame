How to Play Rock Paper Scissors



Rock Paper Scissors is a simple hand game played between two people. The game has three possible outcomes: a draw, a win, or a loss for each player. Despite its simplicity, it involves strategy and psychology, making it a popular choice for quick decision-making or as a simple pastime.


Rules:


    1. Rock crushes scissors (rock wins)
    2. Scissors cuts paper (scissors win)
    3. Paper covers rock (paper wins)
    4. If both players choose the same gesture, the game is a tie and typically played again
